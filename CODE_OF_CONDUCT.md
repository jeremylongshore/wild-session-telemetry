# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the project
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and will take corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.
